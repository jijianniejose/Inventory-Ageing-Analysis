# Inventory Aging Analysis Dashboard (Power BI)


The Inventory Aging Analysis Dashboard is a Power BI project developed to monitor stock performance, product movement, and liquidity risk across multiple branches and categories. It delivers a complete view of inventory efficiency by combining key metrics, visual insights, and drill-through functionality for detailed exploration.

The dashboard consolidates data from gold, silver, and platinum inventory records, categorizing stock by age, branch, and product type. It highlights total stock value, unsold proportions, and movement patterns, enabling business teams to quickly identify slow-moving and high-risk items.

## Key Insights Covered

Total Stock Value: AED 383.53K showing overall inventory holding.

Average Days in Stock: 346.66, indicating aging trends and turnover efficiency.

Unsold Stock Value %: 66.61%, identifying potential liquidity pressure.

Active Stock %: 6.11%, showing currently moving items.

Slow-Moving Stock: AED 104.62K, 27.28% of total value.

Branch-Wise Distribution: Compares inventory across Dubai Main Store, Mall Branch, and Online Warehouse.

Category-Wise Analysis: Evaluates stock and unsold value for items like Chains, Necklaces, Bracelets, Rings, and Bars.

Age Bucket View: Segregates stock into 0–30, 31–60, 61–180, and 180+ days to highlight stagnant inventory.

Unmoved Stock Table: Lists high-risk items with unique IDs, metal type, category, quantity, and unsold value for precise follow-up.

## Dashboard Features

Interactive drill-through pages for category and item-level analysis.

Dynamic visuals including bar charts, KPI cards, and data tables.

Real-time filtering by branch, metal type, and category.

## Purpose
This project aims to help jewelry and retail businesses minimize excess inventory, monitor product performance, and strengthen decision-making on stock replenishment and liquidation strategies. It offers actionable insights to maintain healthy inventory turnover and optimize working capital.
